Rules

Submissions only count if performed in a public match of bomb defusal with real players.
A successful trick performed within 3 seconds of a kill will receive a 2x multiplier. To qualify, the trick must be initiated within 3 seconds of securing a kill, and the kill itself must occur within 3 seconds of the cone landing. Furthermore, achieving 2 kills will upgrade the multiplier to x3, and accumulating 3 kills will elevate it to a x4 multiplier, and so forth.
